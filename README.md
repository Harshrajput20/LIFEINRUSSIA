Russian cuisine is a rich tapestry of flavors and traditions, featuring hearty dishes like Borscht, Pelmeni, and Shchi, influenced by the country's diverse geography and history.
Key Characteristics
Russian cuisine is known for its hearty and comforting dishes, often designed to sustain people through long, cold winters. The cuisine reflects a blend of influences from Europe, Asia, and the Middle East, resulting in a variety of flavors and cooking techniques. Common ingredients include potatoes, root vegetables, grains, meats (such as pork, beef, and lamb), and fish. Sour cream is a staple condiment, often accompanying many dishes.
